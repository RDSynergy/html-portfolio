The Best Movies According to Rob Dean

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas

I like this movie for the thought-provoking nature of the protaganist. It also lends a nostalgic feeling to what life during the late 60's would have been like.

Raiders of the Lost Ark

This is the movie that started it all for me when it comes to learning about history. I came away from the movie feeling like history could be an adventure, and not just a boring classroom environment.

Star Wars

The visual effects from the original movie blew me away as a 7-year old child. It allowed me to imagine the possibilities of one day travelling to a galaxy far, far, away.
